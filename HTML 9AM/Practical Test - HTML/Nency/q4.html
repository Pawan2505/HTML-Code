Harley Davidson Breakout




The Harley-Davidson Breakout 117 is powered by a 1923 cc air-cooled engine which produces 103.33 @ 5020 rpm of power.
It has a fuel tank of 18.9 L and a claimed mileage of 17.85. The Harley-Davidson Breakout 117 starts at Rs 30.99 Lakh Rs (ex-showroom, Delhi).
It is available in one variants.
